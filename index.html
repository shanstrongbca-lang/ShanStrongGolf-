About Shannon

This tournament holds a special meaning as we rally around our beloved boss, colleague, and friend, Shannon DuJack, who was recently diagnosed with breast cancer. Shannon is known for her strength, leadership, and humor. She is facing this challenge with the support of her four sons, close family and friends, and her emergency department family.

This tournament has been organized not only to support Shannon and her family, but also to raise awareness and contribute to the fight against breast cancer. Join us in standing beside Shannon and countless others by turning a round of golf into a swing toward hope and healing.
